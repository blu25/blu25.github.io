Resume

A lowpoly flying game created to learn FMOD Studio. Collect energy from the flowers in the forest to boost your plane and try to reach the end. Watch out for the trees though.

Built in Unity

3 zone triggers check the players position and send it to FMOD

FMOD generates a sountrack that changes based on progression
